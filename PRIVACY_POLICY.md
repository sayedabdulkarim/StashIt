# Privacy Policy for StashIt

**Last updated: January 8, 2026**

## Overview

StashIt is a browser extension that helps you save and organize bookmarks. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

StashIt collects and stores the following data:

- **Bookmark URLs and titles**: Saved when you choose to stash a page
- **Account information**: Email address for authentication (via Google Sign-In)
- **User preferences**: Your settings and organization preferences

## Data Storage

- All bookmark data is stored securely in cloud storage (MongoDB)
- Authentication is handled securely through Google OAuth
- Data is associated with your account and accessible only to you

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal data to third parties
- Share your bookmarks or browsing data with anyone
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Track your browsing history

## Data Security

- All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
- Authentication tokens are stored securely
- We use industry-standard security practices

## Third-Party Services

StashIt uses the following third-party services:
- **Google OAuth**: For secure authentication
- **MongoDB Atlas**: For secure data storage

## Your Rights

You can:
- Access your stored data at any time through the extension
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Export your bookmarks
